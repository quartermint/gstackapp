<context>
The key product insight: the best builders don't use PRs. They ship to main. gstackapp should review every push, not just PRs. This reframe turned a "backfill problem" into a product differentiator — no competitor (CodeRabbit, Qodo, Graphite) reviews pushes to main.

The three-tier token strategy is working: Claude for judgment (CEO/Security), Gemini for pattern matching (budget profile), local LM Studio for free grunt work. Gemini 3 Flash is 3x faster than Claude and produces comparable findings.
